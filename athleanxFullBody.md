Full Body Workout Exercises (ATHLEAN-X)
Workout Structure Overview
Two alternating full body workouts (A and B) using effective reps principle.

Perform ignition set (to failure with 12-rep max weight). 
Rest 15-30 seconds between sets. 
Complete 20 effective reps total (except goblet squat: 40 reps). 
Schedule: A (Mon), B (Wed), A (Fri) → B (Mon), A (Wed), B (Fri). 
Workout A Exercises
1. Incline Dumbbell Bench Press
Target: Chest, shoulders, triceps
Effective Reps: 20
Execution:

Ignition set to failure (12RM weight).
Rest 15-30s, then grind out reps until 20 total effective reps.  
Progression Exercise:



Set 1: 12 reps (ignition)  
Rest 15-30s  
Set 2: 6-7 reps  
Rest 10-15s  
Set 3: 4 reps  
→ Continue until 20 effective reps
2. Underhand Barbell Row
Target: Lats, mid-back, upper back, biceps, low back stabilizer
Effective Reps: 20
Key Point: Underhand grip increases bicep involvement. 
Rest: 3 minutes between exercises. 

3. Dumbbell High Pull
Target: Delts (abduction), traps, upper back
Effective Reps: 20
Execution Notes:

Elbows stay down, hands go high (NOT upright row). 
Wrists above elbows at top. 
4. PJR Pullover
Target: Lats + triceps (long head stretch)
Effective Reps: 20
Execution:

Standard pullover setup.
Bend elbows at bottom. 
Triceps extend first for momentum, lats finish pull. 
5. Goblet Squat (OR Leg Extension)
Target: Quads, glutes
Effective Reps: 40 (goblet) / 20 (leg extension)
Notes:

Use 20RM weight (lighter due to posture limits). 
Leg extension: Avoid if knee issues. 
6. Barbell RDL
Target: Posterior chain (hamstrings, glutes)
Effective Reps: 20
Execution:

Hip hinge (glutes back/forward). 
Bar contacts thighs entire movement. 
Total Time: ~45 minutes. 
Workout B Exercises
1. Weighted Dip (OR Bodyweight)
Target: Chest, shoulders, triceps
Effective Reps: 20
Setup:

Shoulders down (protect shoulders). 
Chest out, good stretch at bottom. 
2. One-Arm High Cable Row
Target: Lats, rear delts, biceps
Effective Reps: 20 per arm
Key Points:

Elbow extension behind body (rear delt growth). 
Supinated grip for biceps. 
3. Cable Lateral Raise
Target: Middle delts (better stretch than dumbbells)
Effective Reps: 20
Advantage: Cross-body pull = assisted stretch. 

4. Barbell Curl
Target: Biceps (direct work)
Effective Reps: 20
Safety: Can drop bar safely when fatigued. 

5. Dumbbell Reverse Lunge
Target: Quads, glutes (knee-friendly)
Effective Reps: 20
Best for: Knee discomfort. 

6. Seated Hamstring Curl
Target: Hamstrings
Effective Reps: 20
Execution:

Heels down to 90°+ flexion. 
Slight hold at bottom intensifies.
